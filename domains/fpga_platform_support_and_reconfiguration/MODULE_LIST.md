# FPGA Platform Support and Reconfiguration

Vendor-facing wrapper IP for transceivers, clocking, startup, and runtime reconfiguration on FPGA platforms.

## Candidate Modules

- `transceiver_reset_fsm`
- `serdes_calibration_block`
- `mmcm_pll_wrapper`
- `iodelay_controller`
- `startup_sequencer`
- `icap_pcap_controller`
- `partial_reconfiguration_manager`
- `bitstream_loader`
- `ddr_phy_wrapper`
- `vendor_ram_wrapper`
- `vendor_dsp_wrapper`
- `transceiver_loopback_tester`
