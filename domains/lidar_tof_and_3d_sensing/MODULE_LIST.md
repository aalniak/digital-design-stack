# LiDAR, ToF, and 3D Sensing

Time-of-flight and ranging support blocks for pulsed lasers, point-cloud generation, and 3D depth pipelines.

## Candidate Modules

- `tdc_capture`
- `tof_histogrammer`
- `peak_detector`
- `multi_return_resolver`
- `walk_error_corrector`
- `laser_trigger_controller`
- `scan_pattern_generator`
- `point_cloud_packer`
- `voxel_filter`
- `timestamp_synchronizer`
- `range_calibration_engine`
- `reflectivity_estimator`
